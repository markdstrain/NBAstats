About

This app is intended to keep track of NBA players. I have scraped data from www.basketball-reference.com so that people can look up individual players stats, salaries, and fantasy league points. It also allows users to add players to a fantasy league type team. It only allows users to track their favorite players as of right now.

Glossary

  • 2P: Two point field goals made
  • 2PA: Two point field goals attempted
  • 2P%: Two point field goal percentage
  • 3P: Three point field goals made
  • 3PA: Three point field goals attempted
  • 3P% Three point field goal percentage
  • +/-: The point differential while the player is in the game
  • Age: The age of the player
  • AST: Assists made
  • BLK: Blocks made
  • Date: The date on which the game occured
  • DRB: Defensive rebounds made
  • eFG: Effective Field Goal Percentage; the formula is (FG + 0.5*3P). This statistic adjusts for the fact that a 3-point field goal is worth one more point than a 2-point field goal.
  • FG: Field Goals made
  • FGA: Field Goals Attempted
  • FG%: Field Goal Percentage
  • FLP: Fantasy League Points; the formula is (PTS + 3P + (-1)*FGA + (2)*FG + (-1)FTA + FT + TRB + (2)*AST + (4)*AST + (4)*BLK + (-2)TOV). This is a popular formula for
       calculating fantasy league points
  • FT: Free Throws made
  • FTA: Free Throws Attempted
  • FT%: Free Throw Percentage
  • G: Games played by a player
  • GmSC: Game Score; the formula is (PTS + 0.4*FG - 0.7*FGA - 0.4*(FTA-FT) + 0.7*ORB + 0.3*DRB + STL + 0.7*AST + 0.7*BLK - 0.4*PF - TOV) Game Score was created by John
          Hollinger to give a rough measure of a player's productivity for a single game.
  • GS: Games Started
  • MP: Minutes Played
  • OPP: The opposing Team
  • ORB: Offensive Rebounds made
  • PF: Personal Fouls
  • Player: The player's name
  • POS: Position
  • PTS: Points Scored
  • rk: Rank
  • S/FL: How much each FLP is worth; the formula is (Player's Salary / Player's FLP)
  • STL: Steals
  • Team: The team the player is currently on
  • TOV: Turnovers
  • W/L Win or Loss
